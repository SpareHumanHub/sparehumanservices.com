Care for Reactive Dogs and Large Breeds

Calm, confident handling for dogs who need extra care.

Reactive Dogs · Large Breeds · Local Care

Reactive dogs and large breeds need patient, confident handling. If your dog gets overwhelmed or anxious around other dogs, new people, or busy environments, a calm routine makes all the difference.

How I approach reactive-dog care

  • Slow, pressure-free introductions to build trust.
  • Calm, consistent handling with positive reinforcement.
  • Route planning to avoid crowded areas and known triggers.
  • Clear communication with you after every visit.

Why large dogs benefit from confident handling

Large dogs are strong and often sensitive. Clear leash control and steady routines reduce stress and create a safe experience for everyone.
