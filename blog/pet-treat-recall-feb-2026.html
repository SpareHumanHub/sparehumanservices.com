Pet Treat Recall Update (Feb 24, 2026)

What pet parents should check and what to do next.

Pet Safety · Local Update · FDA Recall
Summary: On Feb 24, 2026, the FDA announced a recall of Elite Treats LLC Chicken Chips for Dogs due to possible Salmonella contamination. If you have these treats, stop feeding them and follow FDA guidance.

What to check

  • Product: Elite Treats LLC Chicken Chips for Dogs
  • Lot Number: 24045
  • Expiration: 04/2027
  • UPC: 0 73269 27913 2

What to do if you have the product

  • Stop using the treats immediately.
  • Wash hands and clean surfaces that touched the treats.
  • Contact your veterinarian if your pet shows symptoms such as diarrhea, fever, vomiting, or loss of appetite.
